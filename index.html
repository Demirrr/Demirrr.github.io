Biography


I am Caglar Demir (pronounced "cha, la, r"; like in charm, laboratory and role"). Apart from being a brillant son, brother and a partner :), I am an advocate of free public health, free education, anti-racism, and gender equality.

Profession. I work as research fellow at the DICE Group of Paderborn University. Prior to joining the DICE group, I received a bachelor's degree in computer engineering at Halic University and a master's degree in computer science at Paderborn University. During studies, I was fortunate enough to gather industrial experiences of developing and deploying software products to tackle various problems. As a research fellow, my research interest centers around developing scalable algorithms for learning and reasoning on structured data. During my research, I happen to enjoy implementing algorithm from scratch. Through Fundamentals of Machine Learning, I like to share my experiences with others.
