Biography


I am Caglar Demir (pronounced "cha, la, r"; like in charm, lake and role"). Apart from being a brillant son, brother and a partner :), I am an advocate of
- free public health,
- free education,
- anti-racism and
- gender equality.

Profession. I work as research fellow at the DICE Group of Paderborn University. Prior to joining the DICE group, I received a bachelor's degree in computer engineering at Halic University and a master's degree in computer science at Paderborn University. During studies, I was fortunate enough to gather industrial experiences of developing and deploying software products to tackle various problems. As a research fellow, my research interest centers around developing scalable algorithms for learning and reasoning on structured data. The link prediction and the concept learning problems are of particular interest.
