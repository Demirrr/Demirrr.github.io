About me

Caglar Demir is currently employed as a research fellow at the DICE Group of Paderborn University. Prior to joining the DICE group, Caglar Demir received a bachelor's degree in computer engineering at Halic University and a master's degree in computer science at Paderborn University. During his studies, Caglar Demir gathered experiences of developing and deploying software products to tackle various problems. Apart from his studies and industrial experiences, Caglar Demir obtained scholarships granted by Google, AWS and Intel.

Since early 2019, Caglar Demir has been focussing on developing scalable algorithms for learning and reasoning over knowledge graphs. The link prediction and the concept learning problems are of particular interest.

Specialties:

- Knowledge Graph Embeddings
- Knowledge Graph Completion
- Concept Learning in Description Logics
- Structured Machine Learning
